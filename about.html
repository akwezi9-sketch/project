The world of GA.

Godwin Antiedu — known simply as GA — is the photographer and filmmaker behind World of GA and GA RA Art Productions.

The lens moves across studio portraits, match-day sport, music, brand campaigns and the corporate events that matter — in stills and in film, shot with intention and edited with soul.

Every project starts from one line: let’s create memories and bring your visions to life. Whatever the brief, the goal is the same — a frame worth keeping.

01 How it works

Brief to delivery.

Brief

Tell GA the vision — what you’re shooting, who it’s for and the feeling the final frames should carry.

Plan

Dates, locations and a shot list get locked in, so the day runs on creativity instead of logistics.

Shoot

Photo or film production on the day — composed with intention, directed so you can stay present in the moment.

Deliver

Graded, edited and ready to post — finished work built for feeds, screens and walls.
