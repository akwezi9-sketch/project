FAQ Before you write

What do you shoot?

Studio portraits, sports photography and videography, music visuals, brand and commercial content, and corporate events — photo and film, across eight collections.

How do bookings work?

Send the date and a short brief through the form or by email. GA confirms availability, then you lock in the plan together — locations, shot list and delivery.

Do you travel?

Yes — tell us where the shoot is in the brief.

How are files delivered?

Photos arrive as an edited online gallery and films as graded, export-ready files — all delivered online, ready to post.
